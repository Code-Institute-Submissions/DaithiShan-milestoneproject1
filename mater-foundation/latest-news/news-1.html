2 New Units Open in the Mater Hospital

Thanks to our fundraising volunteers, the Mater Foundation are delighted to have granted â‚¬1.07 million in funding to help open 2 new units in the Mater Public Hospital. The new Acute Medical Short Stay Unit and the Hyper Acute Stroke Unit will provide life-saving care for emergency medicine patients. The Hyper Acute Stroke Unit is a 6-bed unit that will provide specialised acute care to stroke patients and others with neurological issues. The Acute Medical Short Stay Unit is an emergency department unit that will transform the care patients receive, with reduced overcrowding, faster treatment times, and designated areas for older and more frail patients. Both of these units will all play a major role in the months and years ahead as the staff at the Mater continue to provide world class care.
