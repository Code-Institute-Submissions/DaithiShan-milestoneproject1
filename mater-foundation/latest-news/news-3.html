2 New Units Open in the Mater Hospital

The Mater Hospital's breast cancer clinic sees approximately 14,000 patients per year and diagnoses around 400 breast cancers. Those are some of the highest numbers in the country. The clinic has been in need of extra capacity in terms of patient consultation rooms, and medical equipment for the past 3 years. However, thanks to the incredible efforts of our volunteers who took on a 30 Day Squat Challenge to do 3,000 Squats in November, a refurb is in progress. Over 350,000 was raised from the challenge and will be used to build 3 new patient consultation rooms, and overhaul the basement floor!
