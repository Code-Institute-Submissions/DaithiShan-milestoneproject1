New Equipment for Cancer Unit

The Mater Hospital is a national centre for cancer care. The Cancer Unit is located on level 7, and sees over 8000 patients every year. As such there is a high level of wear-and-tear on all equipment for this unit. But thanks to the support of our volunteers, we have been able to provide 50 new purpose-built chemotherapy chairs and an equivalent number of chemotherapy pumps to this unit. The chairs are built to provide the best level of comfort possible during a very uncomfortable treatment. Chemotherapy is damaging to the body and can last from 5-7 hours. So small comforts like this make a big difference. And the pumps provide the life-saving drugs at an adjustable level in prescribed measures. These resources are hugely appreciated by our frontline staff, and will greatly help our cancer patients.
