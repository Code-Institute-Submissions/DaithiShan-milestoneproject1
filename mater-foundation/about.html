Our Work in the Mater

Our mission is to advance the care of every patient, every day. Our team of volunteers organise fundraisers, and provide accompaniement services to support hospital care. Read more about the people they help below!

Our vision is of a public, national Irish hospital that provides worldclass care to all, regardless of their means. We aim to achieve this goal by supporting the hospital's strategic plan

Advancing Care Through The Years
