Brendan & Laura's Story:

"I found out I had a rare, genetic heart condition called Long QT Syndrome when my brother died. He was fit and healthy...I'll never forget that call. After the autotopsy revealed it was Sudden Adult Death Syndrome caused by Long QT, I knew I had to get Laura screened. When the results came back, it was every father's worst nightmare. I had given my daughter a life-threatening condition. I felt like I was drowning just walking around. That's when the Mater Foundation came in, their volunteers were there for me and Laura. They introduced us to other families in the same condition. I began to see that the condition could be managed."
